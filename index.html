Aid for Injured in the Quota Movement

The "Aid for Injured in the Quota Movement" initiative provides essential support to individuals injured during protests advocating for job reservations in India. This program aims to ensure that medical assistance, rehabilitation, and financial aid are accessible to those affected. By partnering with healthcare providers and community organizations, it addresses the immediate and long-term needs of the injured.
